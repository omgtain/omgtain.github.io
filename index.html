Who We Are

Hayaikaze is a small preservation kennel located in Western Washington. Our primary focus is breeding versatile companion dogs with health and temperament as our #1 priorities. Our Kai Ken are health tested with the Orthopedic Foundation for Animals to prove their good health. They have also been temperament tested with various AKC programs.
